# Framework

Frameworks provide a structured approach to establishing a [[security_posture|security_posture]] and adhering to [[regulations|regulations]]. For example, the healthcare industry uses security frameworks to comply with [[health_insurance_portability_and_accountability_act_(hipaa)|health_insurance_portability_and_accountability_act_(hipaa)]]. Overall, frameworks allow analysts to work alongside other members of the security team to document, implement, and use the policies and procedures that have been created.
